About NASA Explorer

Welcome to NASA Explorer, an innovative educational platform designed to bring the universe to your fingertips. Built using the powerful NASA GIBS API, this project provides seamless access to high-resolution satellite imagery.

Observe

Explore interactive true-color maps of Earth and other celestial bodies.

Analyze

Dive deep into thermal data and multi-spectral analysis.

Discover

Witness phenomena like Earth at Night through VIIRS technology.

Developed dynamically as an interactive web experience. All images and data are sourced from NASA EOSDIS.
